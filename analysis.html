Analysis of Solutions to Printing

The purposes of analysis to uncover and understand cause-effect relationships, thus providing basis for problem solving and decision making through an examination of data and facts. Common printing problems are studied to make a conclusion on how to choose the solution that fits with our problems.

Analysis tools we use are both quantitative and qualitative

Quantitaive Analysis clicks! At first, we use quantitative analysis to quantify the data and assess it in numbers and other common statistical methods.
Such kind of approach is expected to give the ability to generalize examples.

Qualititaive Analysis Statistics is followed by qualitative research for specifying the studying and the findings more closely. Qualitative analysis will be approached by using cases studies. We intend to study at least three kinds of situations in which why there are printing problems, and study how these problems could be solved by using a particular solution.

  1. MAUTHENTIC - Analysis will not be influenced externally. And hence, it will provide fair and trustful results.
  2. ACCURATE -  Analysis results are expected to be as accurate as possible with more sources used in constructing model for analysis.
  3. INTRODUCTORY - People will be introduced solutions to printing. People will easily know how solutions to printing can help in their daily life. 
  4. RIGHT DECISION MAKING - People will have access to resources required to make decisions on their problem solving.
